About Makumbi Benedicto

Oil & Gas Professional Based in Kampala, Uganda

I am a dedicated oil and gas manager with a strong academic background and foundational industry experience. My passion lies in bridging the gap between traditional hydrocarbon operations and the emerging renewable energy sector.

With a First Class BSc degree in Oil, Gas and Energy Management from King Ceasor University in Kampala, and specialized certification in Petroleum Engineering from Tomsk Polytechnique University, I bring both theoretical knowledge and practical insights to energy challenges.

My graduate trainee experience at Hared Petroleum in 2022 provided me with hands-on exposure to industry operations, complementing my academic foundation with real-world understanding of the sector's complexities.
