Financial Management

Financial planning, analysis, and management tailored for oil, gas, and energy projects

Service Overview

Effective financial management is crucial for the capital-intensive energy sector. My services help optimize financial performance throughout project lifecycles, from exploration and development through production and decommissioning. With expertise in energy project financing, cost control, and revenue management, I provide financial solutions that drive profitability while managing risks.

Services Offered

  • Project Financing: Structuring and securing financing for energy projects, including debt, equity, and hybrid structures
  • Investment Analysis: Comprehensive financial modeling and investment appraisal for energy projects
  • Cost Control & Budget Management: Development and management of project budgets with cost control systems
  • Financial Reporting: Preparation of financial reports for energy projects, compliant with relevant standards
  • Revenue Forecasting & Management: Cash flow forecasting and revenue management for producing assets
  • Tax Planning: Tax optimization strategies for petroleum operations in Uganda
  • Risk Management: Financial risk identification and mitigation strategies for energy projects
  • Mergers & Acquisitions: Financial due diligence and valuation for energy sector M&A transactions
  • Performance Monitoring: Development of financial KPIs and performance monitoring systems
  • Capital Allocation: Strategies for optimal capital allocation across energy portfolios

Financial Analysis & Modeling

Comprehensive financial analysis for informed decision-making:

  • Discounted Cash Flow Analysis: DCF modeling for project valuation and investment decisions
  • Sensitivity Analysis: Analysis of how changes in key variables affect project economics
  • Scenario Planning: Development of multiple financial scenarios for risk assessment
  • Monte Carlo Simulation: Probabilistic analysis for complex energy projects
  • Break-even Analysis: Determination of economic thresholds for project viability
  • Reserve-based Lending Models: Financial models for reserve-based lending transactions
  • Portfolio Optimization: Analysis for optimal portfolio composition and risk-return balance

  • Currency Risk Management: Managing foreign exchange risks in Uganda's dollarized energy sector
  • Tax Regime Navigation: Understanding and optimizing within Uganda's petroleum tax regime

Project Lifecycle Financial Management

Financial support throughout the entire project lifecycle:

  1. Exploration Phase: Budgeting for exploration activities and risk capital allocation
  2. Appraisal Phase: Financial analysis of appraisal results and development decisions
