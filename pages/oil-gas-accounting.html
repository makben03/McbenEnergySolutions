Oil & Gas Accounting

Specialized accounting services for upstream, midstream, and downstream operations

Service Overview

Petroleum accounting requires unique expertise due to joint ventures, production sharing agreements, complex revenue recognition, and specialized cost accounting. My oil and gas accounting services provide the specialized financial management needed for successful operations in Uganda's energy sector. I combine knowledge of international petroleum accounting standards with understanding of Uganda's specific regulatory requirements.

Services Offered

  • Joint Interest Billing & Accounting: Management of joint interest accounting for partnership operations
  • Production Revenue Accounting: Accurate recording and distribution of production revenues
  • AFE (Authorization for Expenditure) Tracking: Management of AFEs for capital projects
  • Royalty & Tax Calculations: Calculation and payment of royalties, taxes, and other government takes
  • Financial Reporting: Preparation of financial statements compliant with relevant standards
  • Cost Accounting: Specialized cost accounting for exploration, development, and production
  • Asset Management: Accounting for oil and gas assets including depletion and depreciation
  • Joint Venture Audit Support: Support during joint venture audits and dispute resolution
  • Regulatory Reporting: Preparation of reports for regulatory authorities
  • Internal Controls: Development and implementation of internal control systems
  • Budgeting & Forecasting: Financial planning for petroleum operations
  • Systems Implementation: Implementation of petroleum accounting software systems

Joint Venture Accounting

Specialized accounting for joint venture operations common in Uganda's petroleum sector:

Joint Interest Billing

Preparation and distribution of joint interest billings to partners

Cash Call Management

Management of cash calls and partner contributions

Operator Accounting

Accounting for operator functions and overhead recovery

Non-Operator Accounting

Accounting for non-operator partners' interests and obligations

Upstream Accounting

Specialized accounting for exploration and production activities:

  • Exploration Cost Accounting: Accounting for exploration expenditures and success/failure determination
  • Development Cost Capitalization: Capitalization of development costs and asset creation
  • Production Accounting: Recording production volumes, values, and lifting entitlements
  • Reserve Accounting: Accounting related to proved and probable reserves
  • Abandonment & Decommissioning: Accounting for abandonment obligations and provisions
  • Farm-in/Farm-out Accounting: Accounting for farm-in and farm-out transactions
  • Production Sharing Accounting: Accounting under production sharing agreements

Ugandan Regulatory Context

Accounting considerations specific to Uganda's petroleum sector:

  • Petroleum Revenue Management: Compliance with Uganda's petroleum revenue management regulations
  • Local Content Accounting: Tracking and reporting on local content expenditures
  • Government Take Calculations: Accurate calculation of government royalties, taxes, and profit oil
  • Currency Considerations: Accounting for multi-currency transactions in Uganda's dollarized sector
  • Community Benefit Accounting: Accounting for community development obligations
  • Environmental Provisions: Accounting for environmental restoration obligations
