Uganda's Legal Framework

Uganda has developed a comprehensive legal framework for petroleum activities that includes:

  • The Petroleum (Exploration, Development and Production) Act, 2013 - Governs upstream petroleum activities
  • The Petroleum (Refining, Conversion, Transmission and Midstream Storage) Act, 2013 - Regulates midstream and downstream activities
  • The Public Finance Management (Petroleum Revenue Management) Regulations, 2013 - Manages petroleum revenue
  • The National Oil and Gas Policy for Uganda, 2008 - Provides policy direction for the sector
  • Environmental Regulations - Including Environmental Impact Assessment requirements
  • Local Content Regulations - Requirements for Ugandan participation in the sector
  • Health and Safety Regulations - Worker and operational safety standards
